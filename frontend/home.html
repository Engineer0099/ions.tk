Descriptions

Headmaster/Principal’s Office

The office responsible for overall school administration and leadership.

  • Oversees school operations and policy implementation.
  • Handles staff and student discipline management.
  • Communicates with education authorities and stakeholders.
Location:

Deputy Headmaster’s Office (Academics & Discipline)

Supports the headmaster by overseeing academic and disciplinary matters.

  • Monitors academic performance and teacher effectiveness.
  • Ensures proper discipline among students.
  • Coordinates school examinations and assessments.
Location:

Academic Office

Handles the school's academic curriculum, lesson planning, and student performance tracking.

  • Manages subject curriculum implementation.
  • Organizes school timetables and lesson plans.
  • Tracks student academic progress and performance.
Location:

Examinations Office

Responsible for the administration of school exams and assessments.

  • Prepares, schedules, and coordinates exams.
  • Ensures grading and processing of student results.
  • Maintains examination records and result archives.
Location:

Library Office

Manages the school library and provides access to learning resources.

  • Supervises book lending and returns.
  • Assists students with research and reading support.
  • Organizes and maintains library materials.
Location:

Sports & Games Office

Oversees extracurricular sports activities and student participation in games.

  • Organizes school sports events and competitions.
  • Manages sports equipment and facilities.
  • Encourages student participation in physical activities.
Location:

School Health/First Aid Office

Provides first aid and basic health care services for students and staff.

  • Administers minor medical treatments.
  • Keeps records of student health conditions.
  • Handles emergency medical situations.
