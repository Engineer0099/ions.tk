Help

1. How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. Follow the instructions to reset your password.

2. How do I contact support?

You can contact our support team by emailing support@ions.com or calling +255 617 772 491.

3. Where can I find the user manual?

The user manual is available in the "Resources" section of your account dashboard. You can also download it here.

4. How do I update my profile information?

You can update your profile information by navigating to the "Profile" section in your account settings.

5. What should I do if I encounter a bug?

If you encounter a bug, please report it to our support team with details about the issue. You can use the "Report a Bug" feature in the app or email us at bugs@ions.com.
